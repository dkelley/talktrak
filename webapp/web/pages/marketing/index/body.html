FAQ

What is TalkTrak and why should I use it?
You don't always have the luxury of speaking to a passive listener. You may face questions that demand immediate and competent responses. TalkTrak gives you the tools to be ready.

Alongside your scripted notes, you can view:

  • Prepared notes and documents for responding to questions you expect, and
  • Helpful hints and documents from your collaborators for responding to the unexpected.

Who is this for? Anyone who speaks to other people and wants to communicate clearly: - Public speakers - Lawyers - Sales representatives - Teachers - Students - The rest of the human race

Is it hard to use?
We don't think so--check out the "How it Works" tab above. There's three basic steps to setting up a TalkTrak.

  • First, simply copy and paste your speaking notes into the app.
  • Second, prep for questions from your audience by leaving notes-to-self with whatever you need to respond.
  • Third, invite your friends and colleagues to your presentation, and they can give you real-time hints and feedback such as "talk slower," or "don't forget to quote ___."
